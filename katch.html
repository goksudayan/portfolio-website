Product introduction

Katch is a US-based early-stage startup with less than 20 employees. At the time I joined the company as the only UX/UI designer on the team, the product was in the private beta stage.

Role — UI/UX Designer

Team — I, Anna B. (Head of Product), and two developers.

Platforms — iOS, Slack

Timeframe —

October 2022 to November 2022

Links — Product website

Objectives

I joined the Katch team as a UX/UI designer...

  • to help the team find their product-market fit by creating very quick mockups and prototypes that can be tested with their users,
  • for anything from sketching to designing, and helping their Head of Product coming up with new ideas,
  • so those deliverables are executed on future live versions.

Outcomes

Throughout my time with Katch, I have...

  • worked closely with the Head of Product, conducting and participating in daily standups and brainstorming sessions,
  • designed a Slack prototype as at the time we deemed it to be the ideal medium to conduct user research and observe user behaviors, though never shipped it, and
  • re-designed several primary features for the Katch iOS app, conducted discussion sessions with the Head of Product and the dev team

Testimonial

"I very much enjoyed working with Goksu for a couple of months when he came on board as a product designer, he was my main partner in discussions about the overall product value proposition. Goksu demonstrated strong analytical skills and could easily grasp the whole user journey from the first day of his work, which really impressed me. Thanks to his user empathy skills he helped us redesign the navigation of the app and worked on a slack app which is still to be implemented.
